KNOW YOUR FOOD

Better Bytes allows you to scan the barcode of a food item product and instantly see if it contains any potentially harmful ingredients. Make more informed choices about what goes into your body every day.

Our goal is to provide users with a quick and reliable way to stay safe by checking the contents of the food products they buy.

Scan a Product Barcode

With Better Bytes, you can simply scan the barcode of any product and instantly receive detailed information about its ingredients. If any ingredients are potentially harmful, we’ll alert you with professional insights, helping you make safer choices. You can scan the barcode using the built-in scanner feature or manually input the barcode number to check the product.

Simply click the "Scan" button to get started with scanning a product's barcode. The system will provide you with information about the product's name, ingredients, and highlight any potentially harmful substances based on expert analysis.

Hear Product Information from a Professional

Not only can you scan barcodes for product information, but you’ll also have the option to hear detailed explanations of the product’s ingredients from a professional. This feature allows you to listen to a specialist who explains the potential risks associated with certain ingredients and suggests safer alternatives if necessary.

To use the "Hear" feature, simply click on the "Listen" button after scanning a product. You'll then be able to hear the information spoken aloud, making it easier to understand the potential risks and benefits of the products you use.
